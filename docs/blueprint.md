# **App Name**: JanSetu

## Core Features:

- Role Selection and Login: Allow users to select their role (NGO Worker, Supervisor, Vendor) and log in with their phone number and OTP verification.
- NGO Dashboard: Provide a dashboard for NGO workers and supervisors to manage beneficiaries and registrations.
- Register Beneficiary: Enable NGO workers to register new beneficiaries by capturing their photo (using Google ML Kit face recognition), GPS location (using Google Maps Platform), timestamp, voice consent, age range, and gender.
- Pending Approvals: Allow supervisors to view and manage pending beneficiary registrations, including the beneficiary's photo, registration details (NGO, location, time), and options to approve or reject.
- My Registrations: Enable NGO workers to view a list of their registered beneficiaries.
- Vendor Access: Provide a vendor interface to scan QR codes or use face verification (using Google ML Kit) to verify beneficiaries before distributing benefits (e.g., rice).
- Beneficiary Active: Display a QR code, JanSetu ID, and status (ACTIVE) for verified beneficiaries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm orange (#FF7043) to evoke energy, enthusiasm, and approachability.
- Secondary color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) for backgrounds to provide a soft, welcoming feel and ensure readability.
- Accent color: Mustard yellow (#FFB300) to highlight key actions and information, creating a sense of optimism and importance.
- Body font: 'Open Sans' for its friendly appearance and excellent readability across various screen sizes.
- Headline font: 'Raleway' for a modern, elegant, and slightly playful feel.
- Use a set of consistent, friendly icons to clearly represent actions and information.
- Employ a clean, organized layout with ample whitespace to prevent visual clutter and guide users through the app intuitively.
- Incorporate subtle animations, like smooth transitions and feedback animations, to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
